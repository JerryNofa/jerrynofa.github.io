Intro

When I joined The GIG Group to work on Stellas digital bank, I also worked on a Banking as a Service (BaaS) platform called Interstellas. The goal was to simplify payment processing for businesses by allowing them to integrate our APIs into their existing systems. With Interstellas, businesses could offer their customers a variety of payment options, including bank transfers, bill payments (such as electricity, cable TV, and internet data), and one-time virtual accounts.

Interstellas offers a remarkable advantage by effortlessly incorporating payment functionalities into businesses' workflows, eliminating the need for extra resources or management responsibilities. By integrating our BaaS APIs, businesses can concentrate on their core operations, all while delivering customers a seamless and stress-free payment experience.

My Role

As the Product Manager I led the development team. Part of my role was to have a thorough understanding of Banking as a Service (BaaS), its end users, their pain-points and goals. To achieve this, I conducted research on other technology platforms in Nigeria that offered similar services. Using the insights I gathered, I created a product requirement document that outlined the scope of the project. Additionally, I designed low-fidelity wireframes that provided a visual guide for the development team.

The product requirement document, flowcharts and wireframes acted as a blueprint for the project and helped guide development towards creating a BaaS platform that was intuitive, user-friendly, and met the needs of end-users.
