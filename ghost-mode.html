Service

Product Management, User Experience Design, Research

Company

Stellas | GIG Group

Year

Overview

At Stellas, I spearheaded the development and introduction of a revolutionary concept called "Ghost Mode." This disruptive innovation is redefining the digital banking industry by offering anonymous money transfers that prioritize customer protection and identity confidentiality. With "Ghost Mode," Stellas customers can securely conduct online transactions while remaining shielded from potential fraudsters.

I defined product requirements, designed the flowchart, and architected the technology. I meticulously crafted a roadmap with clear milestones, ensuring seamless integration. Collaborating closely with the design team, I prioritized usability and security, setting high standards for the concept's implementation. Throughout the process, I conducted rigorous testing and user acceptance validation.

I take immense pride in having developed "Ghost Mode" to fully comply with the Central Bank of Nigeria's regulations, making it seamlessly integrated into the country's banking landscape. With its potential for global adoption, "Ghost Mode" has the capacity to transform the worldwide money transfer landscape and establish me as a visionary pioneer.

Confidentiality Note

Due to the proprietary nature of the concept and its strategic importance as a major selling point for Stellas Digital Bank, I regret that I am unable to disclose any technical details or implementation processes related to "Ghost Mode".

Imapct of feature

Within the first year of launch, Stellas facilitated over 60,000 Ghost Mode transfers, which account for approximately 9% of the total transaction volume. Additionally, these transfers have a total value of around £3.4 million, representing approximately 10% of the total transaction value processed by Stellas.
