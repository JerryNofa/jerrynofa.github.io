Intro

Stellas is a digital bank that has simplified digital banking in the fintech space in Nigeria. Stellas previously operated as a microfinance bank that mainly provided loans to small business holders and conducted routine banking operations through walk-in services. However, with the goal of expanding its reach and improving its offerings, I was enlisted to help transition Stellas into a digital bank, harnessing technology to modernize services and cater to a broader clientele.

My Role

As the Product Manager at Stellas Digital Bank, I led the development of all Stellas product suits from ideation to research and screening, development, testing, and launch.

In developing these products, I:

  • Led the product and operations functions by championing the discovery/user research process, building an agile roadmap to drive customer adoption, retention and growth.
  • Created a profitable product line through a strategic blend of R&D experimentation and collaborations.
  • Developed unique features and experiences that gave the business a unique selling proposition.
  • Innovated Ghost Mode, a unique money transfer feature tailored to keep the sender's identity confidential and protected while also complying with all relevant regulations and laws, such as anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing laws.
  • Created an API product for fintech businesses to seamlessly integrate, collect payments, offer wallet services and disburse money.
  • Developed internal tools (Back offices) for supporting Stellas customers and improving business operations.
  • Implemented a referral program, debit card request, delivery and management feature.

Physical Debit Card

I spearheaded the launch of Stellas' debit card on our mobile app, collaborating closely with my team to develop and implement an architecture that fulfilled my specifications. Working alongside the design team, we conducted research and user interviews, refining the design iteratively to create a user-friendly solution that enhances engagement.

As the project lead, I defined project requirements and oversaw its progress, ensuring a successful launch. I maintained close collaboration with the design team to align our solution with customer needs and preferences, incorporating their feedback and suggestions to refine the design.

Ultimately, my work resulted in a successful launch of the Stellas debit card on the mobile app, which has allowed our customers to easily request, activate, and manage their cards from their smartphones. The launch also met our project goal to increase engagement on the Stellas app and drive up transaction volume.
